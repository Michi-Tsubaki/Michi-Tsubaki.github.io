Biography

Recent Activities

I am currently an undergraduate student at The University of Tokyo, majoring in Robotics Engineering. My research focuses on autonomous systems, computer vision, and robotic manipulation with applications in medical robotics and human-robot interaction.

I am actively involved in developing innovative robotic solutions that bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications in everyday life.

Personal History

  • 2022--Present: Bachelor of Engineering, Department of Mechano-Informatics, Faculty of Engineering, The University of Tokyo
  • 2022--2024: Natural Science I, College of Arts and Sciences, The University of Tokyo
  • 2018--2021: High School Diploma, Eiko Gakuen High School, Sophia School Corporation
  • 2015--2018: Junior High School, Eiko Gakuen Junior High School
